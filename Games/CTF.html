Capture the Flag:

Directions:

Press the green falg

Press space to start the game

Red uses WASD controls

Blue uses Arrow controls

You can hold your flag and return it to your base, but if you cannot take an enemy flag unless they have your flag, or you it is at your base.

If you drop the enemy flag while they have your flag, then they drop your flag

The grey areas will kill you and you will be sent to the corner with a respawn timer

Troubleshooting:

Can't move or space won't work:

Try clicking towards the center of the game screen
