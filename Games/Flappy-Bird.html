Color Thing:

Directions:

Press the green flag

Watch as the center causes color to spread out

You can press space to clear the screen

Press the red octagon to stop the animation

Troubleshooting:

Screen doesn't clear:

Try clicking towards the center where the colored lines come from

Games-Color Thing

Flappy Bird:

Press space to fly

You can also click on the game screen to fly

Troubleshooting:

If the space button doesn't make you fly, click on the game screen

If the game doesn't load, switch to a desktop if on mobile or enable flash on desktop
